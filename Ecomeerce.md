Create a **modern basic e-commerce website** with a **minimal yet luxurious design style**.

**Business Details:**
Business Name: Everopen Innovation Pvt. Ltd.

**Products:**
The store will sell **electronic goods and leather bags with related accessories**.

**Product Categories:**

* Leather Bags
* Electronic Goods
* Home Electronics (AC, Washing Machines, etc.)

**Initial Product Count:**
The website should support **10–20 products initially**, but the structure should allow easy expansion.

---

### Website Pages

1. Home Page
2. Shop / Products Page
3. Product Details Page
4. Cart Page
5. Checkout Page
6. About Us Page
7. Contact Page
8. Category Page

---

### Design Theme

* **Style:** Minimal, clean, and luxurious
* **Color Palette:**

  * Primary: White
  * Secondary: Black
  * Accent: Grey shades
* **Typography:** Elegant modern fonts (such as Inter, Poppins, or Playfair for headings)
* **Layout:** Spacious layout with good white space for a premium feel
* **UI Elements:**

  * Smooth hover effects
  * Clean product cards
  * Elegant buttons with subtle animations
  * High-quality product image display

---

### Core Features

* Modern product listing grid
* Product images, price, and description
* Product detail page with gallery
* Add to Cart functionality
* Cart page with product summary
* Basic checkout page
* Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)

---

### Customer Features

* Browse products by category
* View product details
* Add items to cart
* View cart before checkout

---

### Additional Requirements

* Placeholder product images if not available

---

### Preferred Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js

Design should feel similar to **modern luxury brand stores** with a **minimal aesthetic, strong typography, and elegant product presentation**.
